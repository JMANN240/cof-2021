How can we help?

My pictures aren't showing up!

If your pictures aren't showing when you click on the pictures button, you may need to tell your computer where your pictures are. To do this, click the home button, then click on the settings button in the bottom right corner. Where it says pictures path, click on the "select" button. Then you will need to find your pictures folder and hit select in the bottom right corner of the pop-up.

The print button isn't working?

If your print button isn't working, you might need to tell the computer what printer to use. To do this, click on the home button and then on the settings button in the bottom right. Then click the box next to "default printer" and select your printer.

How do I get to Google?

To get to Google, click on the home button and then click on the search button. This will bring you to Google.
